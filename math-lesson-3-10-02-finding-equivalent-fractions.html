Vocabulary

To find an equivalent fraction, multiply the numerator and the denominator by the same number. 12 × 33 = 36. Both numbers got multiplied by 3. The strip shows why this works — same shaded length, just more cuts.

Problem

Hazel has 23 of a candy bar shaded. She wants to rename it in sixths. What equivalent fraction does she get?
