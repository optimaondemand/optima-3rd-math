Vocabulary

Quadrilateral — any closed shape with exactly 4 straight sides.

Parallelogram — opposite sides are parallel and equal length.

Rectangle — a parallelogram with 4 right angles.

Rhombus — a parallelogram with 4 equal sides.

Square — a rectangle AND a rhombus — 4 equal sides AND 4 right angles.

Trapezoid — exactly one pair of parallel sides.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

All quadrilaterals have 4 sides. The special ones have extra properties: parallel sides, equal sides, right angles. A square has ALL the special properties — 4 equal sides, 4 right angles, 2 pairs of parallel sides. It’s the most special quadrilateral.
