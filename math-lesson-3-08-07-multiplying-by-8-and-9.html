Essential Question
What is 9 × N?

9 × N means N rows of 9 dots. The total is the product. Add another row, the product jumps by 9.

Key idea

An array is a rectangle of dots in equal rows. 9 × N means N rows of 9 dots. The total is the product. Skip-counting by 6 lands on the same product as multiplying.

🔢

How the Array Builder works. Pick a factor (1 to 10). The widget builds an array with that many rows of 6 dots. The equation shows 6 × N = product. The number line below shows the same count as skip-count jumps of 6.

📐 What You Just Saw

Each new row adds 6 to the total.

9 × N = N rows of 9 = 9 + 9 + … (N times) = 9, 18, 27, … N skips on the number line.
