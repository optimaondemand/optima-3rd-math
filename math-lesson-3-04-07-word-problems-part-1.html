Today's Mission
Read a story problem, draw a bar model, decide × or ÷, and solve.
❓
Essential Question
× or ÷?

If you know the size of each group and how many groups, you MULTIPLY to find the total.

If you know the total and need to find group size or number of groups, you DIVIDE.

Warm-Up: × or ÷?

(a) "Atlas saves $5 a week. How much in 7 weeks?" — which operation?

Multiply
$5 × 7
Divide
$5 ÷ 7

(b) "28 children divided into teams of 4. How many teams?" — which operation?

Multiply
28 × 4
Divide
28 ÷ 4
Group size known + group count known → multiply for total. Total known + group size known → divide for count.
