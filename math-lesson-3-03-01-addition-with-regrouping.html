Today's Mission
Stack two numbers. Add one column at a time. Regroup when a column overflows.
❓
Essential Question
What if ones add to more than 9?

You can't write "12" in the ones place. So what happens? You REGROUP — bundle 10 ones into 1 ten, and the ten moves to the next column.

This works for every place value, all the way up.

📐 Key Concept

The column-addition recipe:

1. Stack the numbers — line up the ones.   2. Add right to left.   3. When a column adds to 10 or more, regroup the extra to the next column.

The regrouped 1 isn't really "just a 1" — it's 10 ones bundled as 1 ten, or 10 tens bundled as 1 hundred. That's why it always lands one column to the left.

📋
