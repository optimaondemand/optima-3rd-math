Essential Question
What changes with 3 digits?

One more column to share. Start at the hundreds. If a column doesn't share evenly, trade leftovers to the next column on the right.

Key idea

Share from left to right. Hundreds → tens → ones. Trade leftovers down to the next column. The pattern is the same as 2-digit, just with one extra column to handle.

🍯

How it works. Pick a 3-digit number and a divisor of 2. Share each column left to right. When a column leaves something over, trade it to the next column on the right (1 hundred = 10 tens; 1 ten = 10 ones).

📐 What You Just Saw

Left to right. Trade as you go.

Share hundreds → trade if leftover → share tens → trade if leftover → share ones → read.
