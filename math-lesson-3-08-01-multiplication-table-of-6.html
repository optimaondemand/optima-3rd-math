Today's Mission
Build arrays of 6 to learn the ×6 facts. Skip-count by 6s along a number line.
❓
Essential Question
What is 6 × N?

6 × N means N rows of 6 dots. The total is the product. Add another row, the product jumps by 6.

🎯
Today's Objectives
You will…
🔢Build arrays of N rows × 6 dots.
🦘Skip-count by 6 on a number line.
📝Write the ×6 facts from 6×1 to 6×10.

✏️ Why?

Key idea

An array is a rectangle of dots in equal rows. 6 × N means N rows of 6 dots. The total is the product. Skip-counting by 6 lands on the same product as multiplying.

📐 What You Just Saw

Each new row adds 6 to the total.

6 × N = N rows of 6 = 6 + 6 + … (N times) = 6, 12, 18, … N skips on the number line.
