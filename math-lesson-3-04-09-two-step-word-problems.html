Essential Question
What's the middle step?

In a two-step problem, the answer to the FIRST step is the number you need for the SECOND step. Plan both steps before you compute.

Think About It: What's the Middle?

"There are 24 apples and 18 oranges. They're packed equally into 6 bags. How many fruits in each bag?"

What's the hidden middle step?

Find total fruits
24 + 18 = 42
Find apples ÷ 6
24 ÷ 6
Find oranges ÷ 6
18 ÷ 6
✏️ Now Step 2: ___ ÷ 6 = ___

Find the middle. Then finish.

📐 Key Concept

Three habits for two-step problems:

1) Read the story twice.   2) Plan Step 1 and Step 2 BEFORE solving.   3) Check your answer makes sense.
