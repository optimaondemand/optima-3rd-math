Today's Mission
When BOTH the ones and tens overflow, trade ones first, then tens. Two trades.
❓
Essential Question
What if both columns overflow?

Trade in order: ones first (10 ones → 1 ten), then tens (10 tens → 1 hundred). After both trades, no column has 10+. The mat tells you the answer.

📐 What You Just Saw

Two trades in order: ones, then tens.

4 × 38 = 4 × 30 + 4 × 8 = 120 + 32 = 152

The "carry" from the ones trade joins the tens BEFORE you trade tens. The mat keeps everything in order.
