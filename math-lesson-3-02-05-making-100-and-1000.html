📐 Key Concept

To find a partner that makes 100 (or 1,000):

Jump from the number to the next friendly ten (or hundred). Then jump to 100 (or 1,000). Add the jumps.

It's the same count-up strategy from before — just with a round target. The pair you find is also the answer to "100 − that number" or "1,000 − that number."

Worked Example: 220 + ___ = 1,000
Same recipe, scaled to thousands.
