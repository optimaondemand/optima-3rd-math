Today's Mission
Decide if a number is even (pairs perfectly) or odd (one left over).
❓
Essential Question
Does it split in two?

A number is EVEN if you can divide it into two equal groups with nothing left over (and equivalently: it pairs up perfectly). It's ODD if dividing by 2 leaves a remainder of 1.

📐 Key Concept

Two ways to tell:

1. Pair them. All paired = EVEN. One left over = ODD.   2. Check the ones digit. 0/2/4/6/8 = EVEN. 1/3/5/7/9 = ODD.

Sum patterns: even + even = even. odd + odd = even. even + odd = odd.

📋
