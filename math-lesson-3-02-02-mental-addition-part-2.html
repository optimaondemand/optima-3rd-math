📐 Key Concept — What's the Same, What's New

Same four steps as yesterday.

1. Break apart.  ·  2. Add the hundreds.  ·  3. Add the tens.  ·  4. Combine.

The new wrinkle: When the tens add up to a hundred or more (like 90 + 50 = 140), that extra hundred just joins the hundreds when you combine. It's not a mistake — it's part of the answer.
