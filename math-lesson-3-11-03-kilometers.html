📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

1 km = 1,000 m. To convert kilometers to meters, multiply by 1,000. To find how many kilometers are in a big number of meters, think: “How many thousands?” The leftover is extra meters. 2,500 m = 2 km 500 m.
