Vocabulary: Three Comparing Shortcuts

Same denominator — same bottom means same-size pieces. Compare the tops: more shaded pieces wins.

Same numerator — same top means the same count of pieces. Compare piece sizes: bigger pieces (smaller denominator) wins.

Benchmark comparison — compare each fraction to 12. One past half and one short? The one past half is bigger.
