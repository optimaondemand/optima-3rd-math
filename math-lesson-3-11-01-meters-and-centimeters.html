Vocabulary

Centimeter (cm) — a small unit of length. About the width of your pinky fingernail.

Meter (m) — a larger unit of length. 1 meter = 100 centimeters. About the width of a door.

Convert — to change from one unit to another. 2 m = 200 cm.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

1 meter = 100 centimeters. To convert meters to centimeters, multiply by 100. To find how many meters are in a big number of centimeters, think: “How many hundreds?” The leftover is extra centimeters. 250 cm = 2 m 50 cm.
