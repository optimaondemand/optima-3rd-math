Vocabulary

Gram (g) — a small unit of mass. A paperclip weighs about 1 g.

Kilogram (kg) — a larger unit of mass. 1 kg = 1,000 g. A full water bottle weighs about 1 kg.

Mass — how much matter is in an object (how heavy it is).

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

1 kg = 1,000 g. Same pattern as km/m and L/mL — the bigger unit always has 1,000 of the smaller unit inside it. To convert kilograms to grams, multiply by 1,000. To find how many kilograms are in a big number of grams, think: “How many thousands?”
