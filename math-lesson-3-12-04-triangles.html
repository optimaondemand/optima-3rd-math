Vocabulary

Triangle — a closed shape with exactly 3 straight sides and 3 angles.

Side — a straight line segment that forms part of a shape’s boundary.

Vertex — a corner point where two sides meet. A triangle has 3 vertices.

Closed shape — a shape whose sides connect with no gaps.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

A triangle is a closed shape with exactly 3 straight sides and 3 angles. Every triangle has 3 vertices (corners). Triangles can look very different from each other, but they all share those three features: 3 sides, 3 angles, closed.
