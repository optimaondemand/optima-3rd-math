Essential Question
Why is 12 the same as 24?

When you cut every piece in half, the shaded part stays the same length. The numerator doubles. The denominator doubles. The fraction has a new name, but it's still the same amount of the whole.

Vocabulary

Equivalent fractions are different names for the same amount of a whole. 12 and 24 and 48 are all equivalent. The pieces look different, but the shaded part is the same length.
