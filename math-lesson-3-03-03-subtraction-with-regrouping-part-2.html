Today's Mission
Stack the numbers. When you need to borrow from a zero, cascade the borrow left through the zeros.
❓
Essential Question
How do you borrow from a zero?

You can't take 1 from 0 — there's nothing there. So the borrow has to TRAVEL LEFT until it finds a column with something to give. As it passes through each zero, that zero becomes a 9.

It looks like a chain reaction. That's a cascade.

🎯

📐 Key Concept

The cascade rule:

Find the first NON-ZERO column to the left. Drop its digit by 1. Every zero between there and your starting column becomes a 9. The starting column gains 10.

The total still hasn't changed — it just looks different. 4,005 is the same as 3 thousands + 9 hundreds + 9 tens + 15 ones (3,000 + 900 + 90 + 15 = 4,005 ✓).
