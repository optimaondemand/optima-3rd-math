Today's Mission
Add two-digit numbers in your head — by breaking each one into tens and ones, then putting them back together.
❓
Essential Question
How do you add two numbers in your head?

You don't always need paper. If you can break a number into tens and ones, you can add it the easy way: tens to tens, ones to ones.

Today: one simple strategy, used over and over.

🎯

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

Four small steps, every time.

1. Break each number into tens + ones.  ·  2. Add the tens.  ·  3. Add the ones.  ·  4. Combine.

You already know 4 + 2 (= 6) and 2 + 8 (= 10). The strategy just lets you USE those small facts to add 42 + 28.
