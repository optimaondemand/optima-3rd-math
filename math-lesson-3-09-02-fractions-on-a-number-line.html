Today's Mission
Cut the space between 0 and 1 into equal parts and mark a fraction on the number line.
❓
Essential Question
Where does a fraction live on the number line?

Between 0 and 1, the same fraction sits at the same spot every time. The denominator tells how many equal parts to cut. The numerator tells how many tick marks to count from 0.

🎯

Vocabulary

A fraction names equal parts of a whole. The denominator (bottom) tells how many equal parts. The numerator (top) tells how many of those parts you mean. 34 = three-fourths = 3 out of 4 equal parts.

🍫
