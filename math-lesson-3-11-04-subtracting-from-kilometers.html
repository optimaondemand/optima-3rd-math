Essential Question
How do you subtract meters from a kilometer distance?

You can’t subtract 600 m from 2 km until you convert to the same unit. Convert first, then subtract.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

To subtract meters from kilometers: convert the kilometers to meters first (multiply by 1,000), subtract, then convert back to kilometers and meters.

Who Made the Mistake?
Error Analysis

Felix says: “2 km − 500 m = 1 km 500 m.” Cora says: “2 km − 500 m = 2 km 500 m.” Who is right?

Felix
1 km 500 m
Cora
2 km 500 m
✅ 2 km = 2,000 m. 2,000 − 500 = 1,500 m = 1 km 500 m. Felix is right. Cora added instead of subtracting!
