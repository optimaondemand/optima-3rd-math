✏️ What do you think an angle measures?

Two lines meet — and the space between them matters.

Vocabulary

Angle — the opening between two rays that share a vertex.

Vertex — the point where two rays meet.

Ray — a line that starts at a point and goes on forever in one direction.

Right angle — an angle that makes a perfect square corner.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

An angle is the opening between two rays at a vertex. A right angle makes a perfect square corner. Angles can be less than a right angle (smaller opening) or greater than a right angle (wider opening).
