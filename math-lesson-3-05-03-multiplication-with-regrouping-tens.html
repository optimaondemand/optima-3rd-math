Today's Mission
When the tens column overflows, trade 10 tens for 1 hundred to keep the mat tidy.
❓
Essential Question
What if there are 10 or more tens?

Each column on the mat holds digits 0-9. When tens reach 10 or more, we trade 10 tens for 1 hundred. The total value doesn't change — only the way it's stored.
