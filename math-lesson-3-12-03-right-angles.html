✏️ How do you check if a corner is a right angle?

Square corners are everywhere — once you know how to spot them.

Vocabulary

Right angle — an angle that makes a square corner, like the corner of a book or a door.

Perpendicular lines — two lines that meet at a right angle.

Square corner symbol — the small square drawn inside a right angle to show it’s exactly 90°.

📐 Key Concept — What You Just Saw

A right angle is a square corner. You can test any corner with a right-angle checker — if it fits perfectly, it’s a right angle. Rectangles and squares have 4 right angles. Not every shape does.
